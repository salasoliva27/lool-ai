# GTM — lool-ai

### Target segments
1. **Independent optical stores — Roma / Condesa** — tech-forward neighborhood, higher willingness to try digital tools
2. **Independent optical stores — Polanco / Lomas** — higher ticket, more budget, but slower to move
3. **Optical store clusters near universities** — price-sensitive but high foot traffic

### Outreach approach
- In-person visits to stores — demo the widget live on their own inventory photos
- WhatsApp follow-up after visit
- Referral from first converted store to neighboring stores
- No cold email — this market responds to in-person trust
